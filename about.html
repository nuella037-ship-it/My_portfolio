About us

Who We Are

The Raptor is an independent news platform delivering accurate, unbiased, and timely coverage of local and global events since 2022. Our team of 45 journalists operates across 20 countries, bringing you the stories that shape your world.

Founded in 2022 45+ journalists 20 countries 2,000+ articles published

Our Mission

To inform, empower, and connect communities through fearless, fact‑based journalism that holds power accountable and gives voice to the voiceless.

Independence

We answer only to the truth. No corporate or political agenda dictates our coverage.

Accuracy

Every story is rigorously fact‑checked. We correct errors swiftly and transparently.

Empathy

We tell stories with humanity, respecting the dignity of everyone we cover.

Innovation

We embrace new tools and platforms to deliver journalism that matters, anywhere you are.

Our Journey

From a small newsletter to a global newsroom — here's how we grew.

2022

Founded in Brooklyn

Started as a hyperlocal newsletter covering neighbourhood issues. Our first subscriber was a local librarian.

2023

First National Story

Broke a major housing scandal, attracting our first 10,000 readers and our first investigative hire.

2024

Global Expansion

Opened bureaus in London, Nairobi, and Singapore. Grew the team to 30 journalists.

2025

Digital Innovation

Launched our podcast, interactive data visualisations, and a daily morning briefing.

2026

Today

Over 50,000 daily readers, 45 journalists, and a commitment to independent journalism that has never been stronger.

Why Trust Us

Our readers rely on us for integrity, transparency, and accuracy. Here’s how we earn that trust every day.

Fact‑Checked Reporting

Every article is reviewed by at least two editors. We correct errors openly.

No Paywalls

We believe vital information should be free. Our journalism is supported by readers, not ads.

Community‑Driven

We listen to our audience. Story ideas often come directly from our readers.

Ethical Standards

We adhere to a strict code of ethics — no conflicts of interest, no hidden agendas.
